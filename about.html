$ About Me

Hello! My name is Landon. I am a Software Engineer with a multi-disciplinary background in computer science, music education, and software development.

My Background

I'm a Software Engineer based in Dallas, TX, with a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science from Texas State University (Magna Cum Laude, 2023). I also hold a Bachelor of Music in Music Education from Texas Tech University (Cum Laude, 2021).

My journey into tech started in college when I began building my own computers. Eventually, I installed Linux on my desktop and that was the beginning of my love for open source software. Since then, I have been customizing my computer to my liking, which has led me to learn a lot about hardware and software. My family has influenced me to learn and utilize technology from a young age, which has given me tons of ideas for projects that I can build. For example, my brother asked for a Thermostat to be installed in his house, so I built one using a Raspberry Pi and a temperature sensor. All of this started the day I installed Linux on my desktop and I have been hooked ever since.

My specializations include Linux, Python, web development, backend systems, and Raspberry Pi integrations. I have experience with SaaS development, Kubernetes GitOps, and LLM tooling. Recent work spans enterprise signage (Laravel/PHP), k3s home-lab with FluxCD, observability (Grafana/Prometheus), Raspberry Pi HVAC control, and multi-agent LLM orchestration.
