My Background

I'm a Software Engineer based in Dallas, TX, with a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science from Texas State University (Magna Cum Laude, 2023). I also hold a Bachelor of Music in Music Education from Texas Tech University (Cum Laude, 2021). My journey into tech combines analytical problem-solving with creative thinking, allowing me to approach software engineering from unique perspectives.
